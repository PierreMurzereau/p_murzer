About us

Soccer4Other is a social start-up that organizes soccer tournaments to support education through sport in underprivileged areas of the world. We have previously organized tournaments in Montreal, Mexico and France. To foster this social change, we dedicate all of our profits to sport-oriented local charities.

Offering hope and opportunity for a better future is what drives our organization.

Our Mission

Develop opportunities to foster education through sport in unprivileged areas & the local community by making use of our profits.

How?

We organise soccer tournaments for Montreal students and citizens.

All of our profits are re-distributed to charities such as Pour3Points

Why?

We believe in a definite positive social change applied through the use of sports values.

What?

Although we focus mostly on Montreal students, we accept participants over the age of 16. With the ultimate goal of organizing a fun and fair tournament for all.
